Use the “SAVE” button at the bottom of each page to save its contents. All mandatory fields are marked using a "* " symbol.
Tips

    Proposal Title:
  • Provide an appropriate title for your proposal.

    • Proposal Type: There are four options for this field.
  • Long Term (new) are those proposals which require more than one observing cycle to complete. If it is a new proposal, choose Long Term (new) option.
  • Long Term (ongoing) is when the above proposal submitted again for the next or subsequent three cycles.
  • Short Term are those proposals which are submitted for that particular cycle only.
  • Thesis Proposals are those in which a PhD student is involved. If chosen, additional fields will appear in which proposer is required to provide information regarding the name of the student, expected year of the thesis submission, title of the thesis and a thesis summary.

    • Co-Proposers:
  • This is not a mandatory field.
  • Select your co-proposer(s) from the drop-down list.
  • The list contains the users who have successfully registered and created the profile by providing the mandatory information with the DOPSES.
  • If name of your co-proposer(s) is not listed, please inform them to register and create the profile with the DOPSES. They are required to register only once with the DOPSES.

    • Co-PI among Co-Proposers:
  • Choose Co-PI among Co-Proposers selected from the Co-Proposers list.

    • PI/Co-PI/Co-I who will be present for observations
  • This list contains PI and all the Co-Proposers(if selected in the above-mentioned lists).
