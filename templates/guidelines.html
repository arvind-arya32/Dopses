Guidelines for time allocation and data policy Click! 
Please read the following instructions

  • The proposal (or proposals) can be submitted only by the Principal Investigator (PI).
  • All the mandatory fields are indicated by a * symbol.
  • All the Co-Proposers of the proposal are required to be registered with the DFOT Online Proposal Submission and Evaluation System (DOPSES). Principal Investigator (PI) can include names of Co-Proposers only if they are already registered with the DFOT-OPSES.
  • Check the entries carefully in the "Preview" section before submitting the proposal in the "Final" step. Once submitted, no modifications are allowed, A pdf copy of the submitted proposal will be sent to the PI and Co-Proposers through registered e-mail.
  • For any query regarding the proposal submission, send an email to dopses@aries.res.in and for any technical query send to dtac@aries.res.in .
Acknowledgement
